Bio: The team was created in the start of Season 2 with the main sponsor being Cherenkov Vodka. Raven Motorsport

Bio: The team was created in Season 1 originally named De_Automotives that would dominate the start of the season fighting toe to toe with Kronos. But at the half of Season 1 both teams united making Kronos Automotives that had 3 drivers on the podium of the season driving standings, winning without any competition the teams championship. Season 2 started with them separating and rebranding to Raven Motorsport. Their season was not the best, after dominating, they only managed a 5th position on the teams standings without any drivers in the top of any class.

Vapid World Rally

Bio: The team was created in Season 1 by the manufacturer Vapid that named the team Vapid World Rally. They finished Season 1 with the third position in the Class S category and third in the Teams Standings, a good result at the time since the season was mostly dominated by one team. In Season 2, the team finished third in the Teams Class S standings again but only managed 4th place in the drivers standings.

Redwood Rally

Bio: The team started in Season 1, created by the cigarette brand Redwood with plans of dominating the division. They created the Karin Redwood, but objectives were cut short after a big scandal involving their owner and illegal transfers, making them basically quit the rally itself. They only made small appearances, but at the end of the season they managed to pull a second place in the teams points standings, but on the drivers standings finished with a 5th place. They came back full throttle mid-season, renamed to Redwood Rally, finishing in 7th in the teams standings and their best position in the drivers standings was a 6th place in Class A.
